About Us

Welcome to Sunny Shore & Co! 🌞

We’re a small, kid-run creative hobby business spreading sunshine through fun charms and colourful stickers. What started as a passion project is all about creativity, learning, and fun. 20% of our profits go to supporting the Monash Children’s Cancer Centre. Thanks for being part of our sunny community!
